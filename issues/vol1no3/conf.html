CONFERENCE AT TEXAS A&M:
'How is it Played? Performance, Genre, and Meaning'

October 7-9, 1994

Rudder Tower
Texas A&M University
College Station, TX
U.S.A.

Among the speakers will be Mary-Kay Gamel of the University of
California, Santa Cruz, Tim Moore, of the University of Texas in
Austin, Roger Crockett of Washington and Lee University, Perry
Gethner of Oklahoma State University, Les Wade of Louisians
State University Theatre, and Anna Schatz of SUNY at Buffalo.
The keynote address will be presented by Michael Goldman of
Princeton University.

All lectures will take place in Rudder Tower 401 on the Texas
A&M Campus.

Roger Shultz of Texas A&M will direct Fougard's Master
Herald...and the boys in the Forum on Friday evening.

Registration will be $25.00 for the three day conference, or $10 per
day. Students are free. Tickets for the play may be purchased for
$5 each. Registration checks should be made out to How is it
Played? and sent to Jacque Hand, Rudder Tower, 2nd Floor, Texas
A&M University, College Station, TX 77844-1232.

For further information contact:
Elise Garrison
