TAIROV'S PHAEDRA IN KAMERNY THEATRE (MOSCOW)

Episode of accusation of Hippolytus by Phaedra. Hippolytus (fore-left, right side to audience) raises his left hand and bends back. Phaedra (fore-right) points at Hippolytus with her right hand. Aenona stands behind Phaedra watching Hippolytus, with soldiers to the rear of the stage.
