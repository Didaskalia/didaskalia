Mary Louise Hart is Assistant Curator of Antiquities at the J. Paul Getty Museum. Mary became Didaskalia - APA Listings Editor in December 2002 at a meeting of the Drama Committee of the American Philological Association.

The most significant aspect of her museum duties is the development of a theater program for the Getty Villa in Malibu, scheduled to re-open in fall 2005. The theater program will incorporate productions of Ancient Greek and Roman dramas in the new outdoor theater every summer.
