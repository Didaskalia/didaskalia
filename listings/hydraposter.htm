PLAYING TO THE GODS

July 1st-25th 2004

A summer theatre course on the island of Hydra in Greece

[Photos]

This university accredited course, now in its 5th succesful year, offers a unique opportunity in the study and practice of ancient Greek theatre and culture on one of Greece’s most beautiful and unspoilt islands.

With contemporary directors and companies increasingly adding greek classical theatre to their repertory, “Playing to the Gods” offers an intense and lively experience of Greek drama amidst the enjoyments of Greek island life.

Corinna Seeds, the course director, has been theatre director and drama instructor since 1973. She trained in the Stanislavski method at E.15 Acting School and was Artistic Director of the Stirabout Theatre in London for over ten years and Associate Director/Actress at the Theatre Technis in London. She received a Churchill Fellowship and numerous awards for her theatre work. She has been resident in Greece since 1988, where she works as a theatre director, drama instructor and writer/translator.

Guest Tutors include:

Peggy Shannon (Artistic Director Sacramento Theatre Company)
Tony Church (Royal Shakespeare Company actor and director)
Stathis Grapsas (Greek National Theatre)
