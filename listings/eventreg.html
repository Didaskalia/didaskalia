Events for our Listings

Do you have news of interest to our readers? Are you working on a book or production, or organizing a conference? If it has to do with Greek or Roman dance, drama, music, or popular entertainment, we want to know about it. Please contact the Editor with details: Hugh.Denard at kcl.ac.uk.

Listings of performances should include the following information:

  • Name of event
  • Dates
  • Times
  • Venue
  • Booking information

A brief description of the production, and/or web-page address, are also welcome, as are additional information, e.g. about cast, director, translator.
