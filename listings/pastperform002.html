Dionysus by Tadashi Suzuki
30 November and 2 December 2001. Cal Performances at the Zellerbach Theater, University of California, Berkeley Campus, Berkeley, California, USA
30 Novmeber & 2 December 8:00pm and 2 Dec 2001 at 3:00
Adapted and Directed by Tadashi Suzuki
Suzuki's groundbreaking adaptation of Euripides' The Bacchae. This blend of philosophy and stage-craft depicts the conflict between the god Dionysus and King Pentheus of Thebes as a battle between a religious sect and political authority, between a group's need for control and the individual's right to freedom.
Tickets $30/$46.
