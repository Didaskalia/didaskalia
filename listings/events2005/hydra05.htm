Summer Schools

A summer theatre course on the island of Hydra in Greece
Playing to the Gods
July 1st - 25th 2005
School Director: Corinna Seeds
Email: seeds@otenet.gr

[Photos]

This university accredited course, now in its 6th successful year, offers a unique opportunity to study and experience ancient Greek theatre and culture whilst living in a seaside villa on the beautiful and unspoilt island of Hydra. Hydra is unique in that no cars or bikes are allowed and the only means of transport is donkey, boat or foot. A 4-day trip to Athens, as well as excursions to Epidaurus and Delphi are included in the course as well as all meals, accommodation and travel within Greece. Participants not needing university merit points may come for a 10 or 14 day course.

Seminars/Workshops: Myths and Legends, Architecture of Theatres, History ofAncient Greek Drama, Mask-work, Chorus Work, Text Analysis.

Activities: Greek dancing, cookery and language lessons, boat picnics, movement and voice work in Forests of Episkopi.

Performances: Attending theatre, music and dance performances at the Athens, Delphi Epidaurus and Hydra Festivals as well as outdoor film screening.

Guided tours: Acropolis, Museums, Theatre of Dionysus, Roman and Greek, flea market of Monastiraki.Delphi: Sanctuary and Temple of Apollo Epidarus: CostumeMuseum and Theatre.

Course Directors

Corinna Seeds actress, theatre director and drama instructor since 1973, Corinna trained in the Stanislavski method at E.15 Acting School and was Artistic Director of the Stirabout Theatre in London for over ten years and Associate Director/Actress at the Theatre Technis in London. She received a Churchill Fellowship and numerous awards for her theatre work. She has lived in Hydra since 1988.

Peggy Shannon is a theatre professor and vice-provost at UC Davis as well as Artistic Director of the Sacramento Theatre Company since 1998. She has directed at countless professional theatres across the U.S. including the Alliance Theatre, Seattle Repertory Theatre, San Jose Repertory Theatre, the Los AngelesTheatre Center, the Oregon, Utah, Colorado, California, New Jersey and Idaho Festivals.

Guest Tutors include Tony Church (founding member of Royal Shakespeare Company actor and director), Stathis Grapsas (Greek National Theatre) Professor Olga Taxidou, Drama Dept University of Edinburgh, Vassili Boudouris, Director .
