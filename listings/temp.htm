Grass Roots Greeks to Perform Euripides' ORESTES June 7

Euripides' ORESTES, the harshest, most "modern" and action-packed version of the ancient tale of matricide and vengeance, will be read by the Grass Roots Greeks on Monday, June 7, at 7:00 P.M., at the 6th @ Penn Theatre, 3704 Sixth Avenue in Hillcrest (corner Pennsylvania Ave.); NOTE SPECIAL START TIME A HALF HOUR EARLIER THAN USUAL. A forum with the cast will follow. Reservations strongly advised as these readings sell-out fast; suggested donation $10.00; call 688-9210 for reservations.
