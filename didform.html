Survey

APA Committee for the Performance of Classical Texts

Who are we? The Committee for the Performance of Classical Texts of the American Philological Association.

What is our aim? To share information about performance of Greek and Roman texts, both in antiquity and in the present, and to get more people performing one way or another.

What can you do? Let us know how you are interested in performance by filling out this questionnaire (5-10 minutes time). (All of part I, whatever you can of parts II-IV).

How will this information be used? The committee plans to construct a web site for students, classicists, theater professionals and others interested in the performance dimension of classical literature.

Many, many thanks for your help!
