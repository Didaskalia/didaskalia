Dictionary of Ancient Drama

The Didaskalia Dictionary of Ancient Drama was designed and created by William McDonnell Bond in June 2003.

The Dictionary will expand in time to cover a wide range of topics. We hope it will provide a useful reference point both for readers encountering ancient drama for the first time, and for those more familiar with the area.

Readers of Didaskalia can suggest and contribute entries to the Dictionary - please e-mail the editor at: editor@didaskalia.net.
