Ancient Theatre on the Modern Stage

Didaskalia-APA Listings contains many reviews of modern performances. You can send us reviews of productions you have seen using a simple form. We usually publish reviews on Didaskalia-APA Listings within a few days of their arrival.

There are dozens of articles and reviews about various productions of ancient drama in the Journal. Readers can currently browse through these issue by issue. We will shortly be adding an index and a search facility for the Journal which will make more highly targeted searches possible.

See also the Open University's web-pages on "The Reception of the Texts and Images of Ancient Greece in Late Twentieth-Century Drama and Poetry in English" (http://www2.open.ac.uk/ClassicalStudies/GreekPlays.html), which has a searchable database of modern productions.

The Archive of Performances of Greek and Roman Drama at the University of Oxford has a useful web-site which keeps readers up-to-date with developments in the field: http://www.classics.ox.ac.uk/apgrd/.
